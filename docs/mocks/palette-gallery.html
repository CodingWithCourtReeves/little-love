How to use this

You can pick a candidate outright, or take light from one and dark from another — the token vocabulary is shared, so swapping is mechanical. You can also mix accent colors between candidates: Linen's cream canvas with Twilight's sage familiar, for example, would read like a study at golden hour.

What's locked once you choose: accent-user and accent-familiar become brand colors and show up in product chrome, the marketing site, app icon tinting, and the "AI" pill. Everything else is a tuning knob.
